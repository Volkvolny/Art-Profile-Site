Why Character Design?

My love for character design goes way back to when I was really young. I would easily become immersed in the fantasy worlds of cartoons, anime, and video games, it was fascinating to me. They never ceased to carry me through the difficult times and troubles growing up, and they were my gateway to meeting new people and friends. That fascination brought me into the realm of art.

My personal goal since I started illustration and character design has always been to create unique characters that people would love and relate to, similar to how I connected with characters back then as a child, and even today. I've always found that people are much more immersed in stories and animations when they find a character who they can identify with, and as someone who lives so frequently in the fantasy worlds of anime in her art, I know how special those connections are.

As I continue to delve further and further into my character design and illustrative passions, I strive to create those bonds between the viewer of an animation or the player of a video game, and the characters within those shows and games.

My Skills.

Animal Character Design

I'm well versed in both anthropomorphic and feral animal character designs. I have designed species ranging from felines to canines, to dragons and hybrids. I'm always eager to design new species of characters, and I have designed many animal characters for my own illustrations and webcomic project.

Human Character Design

I have continuously worked hard to gain experience in human character design, and I have created quite a few different types of designs from Dungeons and Dragons characters, to your classic high school students and anime protagonists. I am also able to draw both male and female human characters.

Creative and Unique Character Concepts

My imagination is always running wild when it comes to character design, so I'm always at the drawing board trying new styles and designs to create relateable and lovable characters for readers, gamers, viewers, etc. By hiring me you are sure to get the best of my abilities and receive unique characters and designs to fit your next game, comic/manga, or TV series.

Character Illustration

I am always creating really beautiful and whimsical human and animal character illustrations, and I'm constantly learning new techniques and skills to create better backgrounds with nice shading, highlights, and dynamic character poses.
